About the San Diego Tennis Federation

Welcome! The San Diego Tennis Federation (SDTF) is a league founded by LGBTQ members and open for everyone to join from all walks of life.

Through this site, you will be able to learn about the activities, history, and different projects SDTF is behind.


To learn more about how to join, reach out through the SDTF Contact Page.
