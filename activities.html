Activities and Events

Below is a list of a few activites and what days they are typically happening:

Activity Description Day of the Week
Friday Night Doubles Nearly every Friday evening of the year, SDTF holds a two-hour doubles event at Balboa Tennis Club. Each doubles team plays three sets, rotating courts and opponents between sets. To keep things moving, no-ad scoring is used. Fridays 7pm-9pm
Singles Ladder The SDTF singles ladder is a fundamental part of your San Diego Tennis Federation membership and a great way to play more matches, meet new people, and improve your game. When you join the ladder, you are placed in a list of players. Your goal is to play as many matches and accumulate points by challenging people higher on the list Between January and October, followed by a season-ending Master's Cup Tournament, which will finish the complete season in November
Promiscuous Doubles a fun doubles event held at Balboa Tennis Club (BTC) on Sunday afternoons when Team Tennis is out of season. Participants are randomly assigned a partner to begin, so there's no need to bring a partner. As the name of the event implies, you don't stick with the same partner for long -- you change courts each set and get a new partner. Sunday afternoons, 4pm-6pm
